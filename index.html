About

I'm a software engineering student and developer from Portugal, drawn to the systems side of the field, the layers where data has to move reliably between machines and stay correct under failure. Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of distributed systems and blockchain: consensus, hashing, and the plumbing that keeps independent nodes in agreement. I like building the whole thing, from the smart contract or storage node up to the interface a person actually touches.

Votium

17/20 Full-stack

A decentralized e-voting platform that stores ballots immutably on the Ethereum blockchain. Voter codes are hashed with keccak256 before going on-chain, double-votes are blocked at the contract level, and the whole thing runs cross-platform from a single Expo codebase.

18/20 Distributed systems
