About

I'm a software engineering student and developer from Portugal, drawn to the systems side of the field, the layers where data has to move reliably between machines and stay correct under failure. Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of distributed systems and blockchain: consensus, hashing, and the plumbing that keeps independent nodes in agreement. I like building the whole thing, from the smart contract or storage node up to the interface a person actually touches. Lately that also means the operations side: deploying applications to Kubernetes clusters on Google Cloud, automating the whole install with Ansible, and load-testing them until I find the point where they break.

DistributedService

              

                18/20
                Distributed systems
